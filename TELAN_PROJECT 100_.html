Baguio City

Welcome to HighlandLens, a glimpse into my journey of studying and living in Baguio. Adjusting to the city's cold climate and independent life wasn't easy, but Baguio quickly became my home. Through its breathtaking landscapes, cozy spots, and vibrant culture, I found comfort and inspiration. Here, I’ll share the places that make Baguio special—not just for me, but for anyone who wants to experience its charm.

Laser Marketing

Laser is a store in Baguio that offers
a variety of school supplies at lower
prices. From notebooks and pens to
art materials, they provide options
for students looking for
budget-friendly essentials.

Victoria Supermarket in Baguio offers

From fresh produce to household
essentials, it’s a go-to place for
budget-conscious shoppers looking
for everyday necessities.
