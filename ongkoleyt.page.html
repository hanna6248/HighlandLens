Ongkoleyt

Ongkoleyt is a great spot for affordable chocolates and imported goods, perfect for both locals and tourists looking for budget-friendly treats. Located near Victoria Supermart and Laser Marketing, it’s a convenient stop if you're already shopping for groceries or school supplies. With a wide selection of chocolates, snacks, and other imported items at lower prices, it’s a go-to place for pasalubong or personal treats without overspending.
