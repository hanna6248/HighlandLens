Cafe Gia

As a student, finding the perfect place to study outside of home is always a challenge, especially when we don’t have stable Wi-Fi. That’s why Café Gia has become my go-to spot whenever I need a quiet and cozy place to focus on my schoolwork. Conveniently located near the University of Baguio, it’s an ideal space for students like me who need a comfortable study area with affordable food and drinks.
One of the things I love most about Café Gia is its budget-friendly menu. Unlike other cafes where prices can be overwhelming, Café Gia offers delicious meals, coffee, and snacks at prices that fit a student’s budget. Whether I need a quick caffeine boost or a filling snack to keep me energized, I know I can always get something satisfying without spending too much.
Aside from the affordability, the ambiance of Café Gia is what keeps me coming back. The place is warm and inviting, with a relaxing atmosphere that makes studying feel less stressful. The soft lighting, the wooden interior, and the gentle hum of conversations create the perfect balance of peaceful yet lively—just enough to keep me focused but not too quiet to feel isolating.
For students looking for a comfortable and budget-friendly study spot near UB, Café Gia is a hidden gem. Whether I’m reviewing for an exam, working on a research paper, or simply unwinding with a good book, this cafe provides everything I need: good food, reliable Wi-Fi, and a great study environment. It’s truly a student-friendly space where I can be productive while enjoying delicious treats.
